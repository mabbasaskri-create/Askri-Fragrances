Since 2015

A House of Fragrance

Askri Fragrances was born from a passion for scent as memory — a belief that a fragrance should not simply adorn, but define. From our atelier in Paris and our sourcing houses in the Arabian Gulf, every Askri creation is composed by hand.

We source the world's rarest essences: Cambodian oud aged for over a decade, Bulgarian rose absolute, Indian sandalwood, and Grasse jasmine. Each ingredient is chosen with intention, then blended by a master perfumer who has spent a lifetime perfecting the craft.

Askri is not a mass-market brand. We produce our fragrances in small batches, and every bottle carries with it the signature of the artisan who made it. This is luxury as it was intended.
